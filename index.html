How It Works

Simple for Everyone

Whether you're a musician looking for gigs or a venue searching for live talent — we've got you covered.

🎤 For Musicians

Showcase your talent and get booked by top venues.

1

Create Your Profile

Add your name, genres, experience level, hourly rate, city, and bio to stand out.

2

Get Discovered

Venues browse and filter musicians by genre and city. Your profile is your stage.

3

Accept Bookings

Review incoming booking requests with event details, and accept or decline with a tap.

4

Perform & Earn

Show up, play your set, and track your earnings right from the dashboard.

🏪 For Venues

Find and book live talent for your café, restaurant, or event.

1

Set Up Your Venue

Create your venue profile with type, location, capacity, and a brief description.

2

Browse Musicians

Search by genre or city, view profiles, and find the perfect fit for your ambiance.

3

Send Booking Request

Pick a date and time, add event notes, and send a request — it's that easy.

4

Host the Show

Once accepted, you're all set. Track booking status and manage your upcoming events.
